Willow , "Magnus Dominus"

Fearless protector of home and family. Keeping squirrels, socks and avacodos in-check since 2017. 1

  • Scientific Name:Canem dominus magnum familiaris
  • Life Span: 11-14 years.
  • Average Size: 50-58 pounds.
  • Mortal Enemies: Squirrels, socks and avacodos
  • Secret ability: "Able to hold 8 oz. of liquid in mouth and dribble in places you can not possibly imagine"
  • Position: Head of Household, "Primary Food Taster" and leftover disposal expert.
  • Secret Desire: to be a pocket-book puppy for some celebrity

This little cuddle hound was rescued at the age of six weeks from an abusive home. After an early scare and a brief adjustment to her new family she has come into her own.

Willow has cuddled, kissed and wormed her way to become "magnus dominus" of the house. Lording her might over the family cats and local squirrel population.

1 Willow may have the body language of a brave dog but its all an act. Reality is she is a timid but loveable bundle of energy.
